Chocolate Chip Cookies

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making chokolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanile extract
  • 1/2 cup mini chokolate chips

Instructions

  1. prebeat your oven to 320 degree F
  2. In a bowl whisky together the flour and bakingmsoda
  3. In another bowl, beat the butter, sugar and vanilla extract until creamy
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture , then fold in the mini chocolate chips
  5. Drop small spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet
  6. Bake for 8-10 min, then let cool before enjoying
